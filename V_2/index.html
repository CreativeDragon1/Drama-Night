About the Event

Experience a fresh take on the timeless classic "My Fair Lady" as GIIS students bring this beloved musical to life with their own creative twist. This isn't just a performance—it's a celebration of talent, dedication, and community spirit.

Through captivating drama, stunning musical performances, and innovative staging, our students will transport you into a world where language, class, and identity collide in the most entertaining ways possible.

Join us for an evening of artistry and purpose as we come together to support a meaningful cause while showcasing the incredible talents of our student performers.
